A Mother's Love

The hand that rocks the cradle also makes the house a home. It is the prayers of the mother that keeps the family strong.
Mommy rises early in the morning and bathes her day in prayer. She talks to God about her family and places them in His care.
Mommy communicates her love in a thousand different ways. When there's a need, she is there, whether it is night or day.
Mommy seasons life with love and gives so much of herself. God placed in her the best He had and made her unlike anyone else.
When challenges come our way and when trials block our view, Mother kneels down beside her bed and prays the family through.
I love you Mom and really appreciate you. For putting up with me over these 19 years.
